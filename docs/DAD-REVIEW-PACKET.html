1. The business idea

The metaphor

Everyone wants an agent in LA: the person who gets you the best opportunities. Right now, the hot version of that is AI. I become that agent for all things AI.

What I actually do

  • Help clients decide what AI is worth using and what is noise
  • Build or set up agents and workflows when something should exist
  • Stay in their corner as tools, vendors, and needs change
  • Protect them from AI theater and bad decisions

What this is not

  • Not “we build one agent together and goodbye”
  • Not a generic AI consulting menu
  • Not a software product launch (service / representation first)
  • Not free ChatGPT tutoring

Who it is for

Operators, founders, and professionals who want a permanent AI edge and someone accountable for it.

Why now

AI is moving fast. People are overwhelmed. They need representation, not more tabs and tools.

Boardroom sentence

“I become your agent for all things AI. Full-service. Results-driven. Los Angeles.”

3. Brand and visual direction

Inspiration

CAA / UTA / Roc Nation energy: prestige, restraint, representation. Not startup gradients. Not “AI purple.”

Visual system

  • White / black / green only
  • Primary mark: my real handwriting of “The AI Agent Agent”
  • Institutional lockup: clean uppercase wordmark + green rule + Los Angeles
  • Type: clean grotesk (Inter / Helvetica style), not decorative AI fonts
  • Space: sparse, agency-like, lots of room

Color

ColorHexUse
White#FFFFFFBackground
Black#000000Type / structure
Muted#666666Secondary text
Green#0B6B3AAccent / authority only

Voice rules

  • Direct, confident, short
  • No fake authenticity language
  • No baseless claims
  • No em dashes
  • Boardroom test on everything
