# Brand

## Name
The AI Agent Agent

## Domain
theaiagentagent.com

## Core metaphor
LA talent agent, applied to AI.
Everyone wants the best agent. The word on the street is AI agents. Noah gets you agentic.

## Product line
**Results-driven agent.**  
**One session. One change.**

## Voice
Noah's spoken energy, cleaned for the page:
- Direct
- Warm
- Competitive without posing
- Service-first
- Motion: get excited, get moving, get after it
- Results over theater

Short lines. Real talk. No corporate AI soup.

Full kit: `MESSAGING.md`  
Tokens: `DESIGN.md`  
Visual board: open `board.html`

## Visual
- Black and white editorial base (warm paper on near-black)
- Large serif headlines (Cormorant Garamond)
- Body: IBM Plex Sans
- Gold accent `#C4A574` as garnish only
- Moody, not cheerful
- Space. Not dashboard clutter.

## Logo
Monogram **AA** (Agent Agent): two interlocking A strokes, second in gold. Plaque frame optional for avatars.

## Lines that land
- Everyone wants an agent in LA.

## Do not say
- authentic
- revolutionary / seamless / leverage / utilize
- AI-powered (unless forced)
- big agency cosplay
- baseless claims
- unlimited as a vague headline

## Positioning sentence
I'm The AI Agent Agent: results-driven. One session. One change. I make AI your secret weapon.
