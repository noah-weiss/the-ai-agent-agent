# Messaging kit

Everyone wants an agent in LA. Now the word on the street is AI agents. I'm The AI Agent Agent: results-driven. One session. One change. I make AI your secret weapon.

## Elevator (30 seconds)

Everyone wants the best agent: the person who books the best gigs in town. Right now that means AI. Everything is moving fast. I get people up to speed and get them agentic. I don't work for a big studio or a big agency. It's just me. Results-driven. One session. One change. We pick the thing that matters and ship one real upgrade.

## Audience language

**They say:** "I need to get on AI."  
**We say:** "What result do you want? We take one session and make one change."

**They say:** "Can you build me agents?"  
**We say:** "Yes. We pick the highest-leverage one first and ship it."

**They say:** "How does this work?"  
**We say:** "One session. One change. Results-driven. Then we decide if another session makes sense."

## Proof posture

- No fake case studies
- Show the session outcome
- Show the change, not the deck
- Speed is part of the brand (friends already know Noah responds fast)

## Voice checklist before anything ships

- [ ] Sounds like Noah talking, not a SaaS homepage
- [ ] No em dashes
- [ ] No "authentic" / revolutionary / seamless
- [ ] Concrete result language
- [ ] Boardroom test passed
- [ ] Garnish is there (one human detail or excess care)

## Banned

authentic, leverage, utilize, seamless, revolutionary, AI-powered (unless forced), game-changer, synergy, deep dive (as filler), "just checking in"
