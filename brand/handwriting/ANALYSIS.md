# Handwriting analysis — 2026-07-15

Source: `samples/01-the-ai-agent-agent.jpeg`  
Ink: blue pen on white paper  
Five versions of the name on one page.

## Overall character

Confident, fast, a little theatrical. Not neat school penmanship. Signature energy.

Distinctive moves:
- Capital **T** in “The” often opens with a big lead-in stroke (almost 2/Z energy)
- **g** in “Agent” drops a long descender like a signature tail
- **A** in AI sometimes loops / open cursive
- Slight rightward motion, loose spacing between words
- Pressure is even, not scratchy

This hand already feels like an agent’s autograph, not a SaaS logo.

## Versions on the page

### 1. Top line — flowing signature (HERO candidate)
**“The AI Agent Agent”** one horizontal line, full cursive.

- Most complete as a single wordmark
- Highest “this is a person” energy
- Big g-tails on both Agent words
- Decorative The
- Readability: good at large size; needs cleanup for small favicon use

**Use:** primary site hero wordmark, LinkedIn banner, signed closes

### 2. Middle-left stack — logo lockup candidate
**“The AI Agent”** then **“Agent”** dropped under, big final g.

- Best structural logo shape (two-line lockup)
- The second “Agent” becomes a signature underline
- Strong for header / card / plaque hybrids

**Use:** site header, avatar-adjacent lockup, business card

### 3. Middle-right ALL CAPS — stamp / plaque candidate
**THE / AI AGENT / AGENT** stacked block print.

- Least “handwriting brand,” most systemizable
- Architectural, deal-memo, stamp-adjacent
- Good contrast to the cursive when used as a secondary mark

**Use:** Option C stamp language, session receipts, rigid UI moments

### 4. Mid-right small cursive — casual note
**“The AI / Agent Agent”** smaller, looser.

- Intimate, notebook energy
- Slightly less controlled than #1 and #2
- Good as a secondary signature, not the master mark

**Use:** soft sign-off, black-book moments

### 5. Bottom all-lowercase — quiet brand
**“the ai agent agent”** soft, spaced, lowercase.

- Most hospitality / understated
- Feels like a private note, not a billboard
- Beautiful as a footer or whisper line under a stronger mark

**Use:** footer, email sign-off, black-book option

## Rank for brand master

1. **#1 top signature line** — master wordmark  
2. **#2 stacked Agent** — primary lockup  
3. **#5 lowercase** — soft system  
4. **#3 caps block** — utility / stamp  
5. **#4 small cursive** — spare / alt
